After Purchase

Care
Instructions

01
Velvet & Velour
  • Dry clean only — do not machine wash
  • Store hanging to preserve pile direction
  • Steam from reverse side only
  • Keep away from direct sunlight to prevent colour fade
  • Brush gently with a soft cloth if crushed
02
Silk & Silk-Blend
  • Hand wash cold or dry clean recommended
  • Use mild, pH-neutral detergent only
  • Do not wring — press gently in a towel to remove water
  • Iron on low heat with a pressing cloth
  • Store folded away from light and heat
03
Metallic & Embellished
  • Dry clean only — no exceptions
  • Handle with care — embellishments are hand-applied
  • Store in a dust bag, flat or lightly rolled
  • Do not iron directly on embellishments
  • Avoid contact with perfume or oils
04
Wool & Structured
  • Dry clean recommended for structured blazers
  • Air after wearing — do not store in plastic
  • Use a lint roller or soft brush regularly
  • Steam to remove light creases
  • Store on a wide, shaped hanger
05
Cotton & Jersey
  • Machine wash cold, gentle cycle
  • Wash inside out to protect graphics and prints
  • Do not tumble dry — air dry flat
  • Iron on medium heat, inside out
  • Separate darks from lights on first wash
06
Feather Trim
  • Dry clean only — water will damage the structure
  • Store hanging with adequate space around the garment
  • Reshape gently with fingers if feathers flatten
  • Keep away from humidity and direct heat
  • Handle feather trim as little as possible

About
ShazNash

Fashion built on tension — between structure and softness, storm and stillness.

ShazNash is a Kuala Lumpur-based fashion house designing garments that exist at the intersection of architecture and emotion. Founded with a singular creative vision, the brand refuses to separate craft from concept.

The debut collection, Under The Weather, premiered at Kuala Lumpur Fashion Week 2025 — immediately positioning ShazNash among Malaysia's most compelling emerging voices. Each piece is built by hand, with obsessive attention to structure, texture, and the way fabric holds space.

We design for those who understand that getting dressed is an act of intention.

Shipping &
Policies

Shipping

ShazNash ships within Malaysia and internationally. All orders are processed within 3–5 business days. Ready-to-wear pieces are dispatched from Kuala Lumpur.

  • West Malaysia — RM 15 flat rate · Free shipping on orders above RM 500
  • East Malaysia (Sabah & Sarawak) — RM 25 flat rate
  • Singapore & Brunei — RM 45 · 5–10 business days
  • International — calculated at checkout · 10–21 business days
All orders are shipped via registered courier with tracking. You will receive a tracking number via WhatsApp or email once your order is dispatched.

ShazNash is not responsible for delays caused by customs clearance for international shipments. Import duties and taxes are the responsibility of the recipient.

Returns & Exchange

We want you to love your ShazNash piece. If something isn't right, we are here to help.

  • Returns accepted within 7 days of delivery for ready-to-wear items
  • Item must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with all tags attached
  • Sale items and custom orders are final sale — no returns or exchanges
  • Exchanges are subject to size availability

To initiate a return, contact us via WhatsApp with your order details. Return shipping costs are borne by the customer unless the item is defective.

Refunds will be processed within 7–14 business days upon receipt and inspection of the returned item.
Custom Orders

All custom orders are made to order and are non-refundable. Each piece is crafted specifically for the client based on agreed measurements and design direction.

  • 50% deposit required to commence production
  • Remaining balance due upon completion, before delivery
  • Production timeline: 4–8 weeks depending on complexity
  • Alterations within agreed scope are included in the quoted price
All custom order clients receive a dedicated consultation — in person at our Kuala Lumpur studio or virtually.
Payment

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Online Banking / FPX — all Malaysian banks supported
  • Touch 'n Go eWallet
For custom orders, payment is in two stages — 50% deposit to begin, 50% upon completion.
Privacy Policy

ShazNash collects personal information solely for the purpose of fulfilling your order or enquiry. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

  • Payment information is processed securely through our payment partners
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time
  • By placing an order, you consent to being contacted via WhatsApp or email

For privacy concerns, contact hello@shaznash.com.
